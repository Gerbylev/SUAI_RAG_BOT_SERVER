October 21

Create with Wisdom: SUAI hosted a conference on artificial intelligence

The second International Conference on Applied Artificial Intelligence: Prospects and Risks was held at St. Petersburg State University of Aerospace Instrumentation

October 18

Space is closer than you think!

The X Anniversary All-Russian Cosmonautics and Aviation Forum «KosmoStart» was held in St. Petersburg from October 16 to 18. This year’s annual forum is dedicated to the 10th anniversary of its founding.

October 9

SUAI developments were presented to the Director General of the Roscosmos State Corporation

On October 9, Dmitry Bakanov, Director General of the Roscosmos State Corporation, visited St. Petersburg State University of Aerospace Instrumentation.

October 9

Remote sensing of the Earth as a key to environmental and technospheric security

On October 9 an interuniversity meeting on technologies for collecting, processing and storing data obtained as a result of remote sensing of the Earth was held in SUAI.

September 19

SUAI experts develop a unique color recognition device

A device developed in the SUAI Laboratory of Automatic Systems has it’s functionality similar to industrial devices. The object color meter does not just capture shades, it instantly transmits data to the industrial RS-485 network via the Modbus RTU protocol in real time.

September 5

An article about a research by SUAI scientists published in the world’s leading scientific journal.

The article is devoted to a comprehensive experimental study of the spectroscopic characteristics of a multisectoral plate of synthetic HPHT diamond with nitrogen vanishing NV color centers.
