09.03.02 Information Systems and Technologies

Three Majors are available
Information Technology in Design: The program is aimed at training highly qualified specialists in the field of development, implementation and application of information systems and technologies to solve problems in the field of design. The degree holder receives a sufficient amount of professional knowledge, skills and abilities to work in positions requiring higher education and competencies in areas such as computer graphics and image processing, 3D scene modeling, graphic and UI/UX design, web design, programming in Python, C++, C#, Java, JavaScript, Kotlin, IP modeling, database design, using AI methods, visualization and big data analysis, technical documentation in the field of IT.

Information Technology in Media Industry: The program is aimed at training specialists in the field of development, implementation and maintenance of information technologies in the media industry. The degree holder receives a sufficient amount of professional knowledge, skills and abilities to work in positions requiring higher education and competencies in areas such as development of Web applications and services, graphic design and 3D modeling, programming, the use of artificial intelligence methods, visualization and analysis of big data, the basics of game development, as well as the theory of information transmission and protection.

Information Systems and Technologies in Business The program is aimed at training specialists in the field of development, implementation and maintenance of information systems and technologies in business processes. The degree holder receives a sufficient amount of professional knowledge, skills and abilities to work in positions requiring higher education and competencies in areas such as business analytics, management, Internet marketing, advertising, administration in information systems, the Internet of Things, information system design, web and multimedia technologies, programming, the use of artificial intelligence methods, visualization and analysis of big data, collection, processing and storage of information.

09.03.03 Applied Informatics

The program is aimed at training highly qualified specialists who are proficient in a wide range of tools, familiar with all types of software, proficient in all types of modern computer technologies: object-oriented, system, functional, logical, visual programming, graphical systems, artificial intelligence systems, etc.

09.03.04 Software Engineering

The program focuses on the methodology of designing software systems for various applications, including the fields of rocket and space technology. Competencies related to the analysis, formalization of knowledge, artificial intelligence systems, neural networks, big data technologies and other modern trends are being implemented.

A special feature of the educational program in Software Engineering is its focus on software design technologies using modern software engineering methodology, which allows graduates to quickly adapt to the specifics of working in enterprises of the real sector of the economy.

 170000
11.03.03 Instrumentation and Electronic Means Technology

The program is focused on training specialists in the field of programming, computer systems and networks design, design of central and peripheral devices of computer systems, design and technology of microsystems engineering and nanotechnology.

Bachelor’s degree holder receives a sufficient amount of professional knowledge, skills and abilities for employment in positions requiring higher education and competencies in areas such as complex electronic computing systems, their designs and technologies.

 170000
12.03.04 Biotechnical Systems and Technologies

The program is aimed at training highly qualified specialists in the field of design, metrological control, repair and maintenance of medical equipment. The training is conducted at the interface of two areas — technical and biological (medical). The list of subjects studied includes natural sciences and engineering disciplines.

 170000
15.03.04 Automation of Technological Processes and Productions

During the training process, students acquire knowledge in such areas as Electrical Engineering, Analog and Digital Electronics, Fundamentals of Automation and Computerization, Design of Control Devices, Programming, Circuit Design, Control and Diagnostics.
The degree holder receives a sufficient amount of professional knowledge, skills and abilities to work in positions requiring higher education and competencies in areas such as systems and means of control of technical objects; automation and mechanization of production processes; automation and control in production; electronic engineering; system engineering; control and computer science in technical systems; software engineering; technical support.

 170000
15.03.06 Mechatronics and Robotics

The major available: Digital Engineering of Robotic Systems. The program aims to train specialists with knowledge and skills in the development, design, operation, and maintenance of robotic systems and complexes.
The degree holder receives a sufficient amount of professional knowledge, skills and abilities to work in positions requiring higher education and competencies in areas such as machinery and equipment production (in the areas of automation and mechanization of production, increasing productivity and labor safety); cross-cutting types of professional activity in the field of conducting research and development in the industry.

20.03.01 Technosphere Safety

The program is aimed at training specialists for work in high-tech industries, capable of implementing and competently using innovative information technologies and digital methods of managing technosphere and environmental safety.

Graduates of the educational program receive a unique set of engineering and digital competencies for forecasting, preventing and eliminating man-made hazards and risks using information technologies, including intellectual ones.

23.03.01 Transport Processes Technology

Major available: Transportation Organization and Management in a Unified Transport System. The program is aimed at training highly qualified specialists in the field of the theory of cargo science, methods of routing, the theory of transport processes and systems, the safety of transport processes, the theory of passenger transportation, models and methods for supply chains and methods of their management, aspects of the operation of warehouse systems, transport systems and terminals. During the training period, students have an opportunity to study practical issues in relation to various modes of transport.
The degree holder receives a sufficient amount of professional knowledge, skills and abilities to work in positions requiring higher education and competencies in areas such as organization of passenger transportation; organization of cargo transportation; management of transportation processes; generation of transport documentation; organization of international transportation; management of product movement in supply chains; ensuring the reliability of transportation processes and systems; routing of vehicles; transport engineering, maintenance, and repair; organization of passenger transportation by various modes of transport; working with and developing information logistics software.

 170000
25.03.01 Technical Exploitation of Aircrafts and Engines

The program is focused on design and research activities in the field of development and maintenance of aircraft systems. Students learn how to maintain aviation equipment, monitor the flight readiness of equipment, compile technical documentation, prepare technical facilities for certification, conduct experiments and analyze their results. The following subjects are fully taught as a theoretical basis: theoretical mechanics, engineering and computer graphics, and aerodynamics. 

Bachelor’s degree holder receives a sufficient amount of professional knowledge, skills and abilities for being employed by airlines, airports, aircraft repair plants, flight test stations and research institutes of the aviation industry.

40.03.01 Legal Studies

Majors available:
Civil Law: Graduates of the program can carry out professional activities in the areas of developing and implementing legal norms, ensuring law and order, and providing legal assistance to individuals and legal entities.

Criminal Law: Graduates of the program can carry out professional activities in such areas as: substantiation and decision-making within the scope of official duties, as well as actions related to the implementation of legal norms; drafting legal documents; protection of private, state, municipal and other forms of ownership; legal advice; formation of a unified law enforcement practice that promotes a uniform interpretation of legal norms; selection of a legal norm for determining the behavior of participants in a legal relationship or for evaluating behavior; preparation of draft regulations and local regulations, as well as draft amendments and additions to these acts.

State Law: Graduates of the program can carry out professional activities in such areas as: substantiating and making decisions within the scope of official duties, as well as performing actions related to the implementation of legal norms; drafting legal documents; protecting private, state, municipal and other forms of ownership; advising on legal issues; forming a unified law enforcement practice that promotes a uniform interpretation of legal norms; choosing a legal norm to determine the behavior of participants in a legal disputeto evaluate behavior; preparation of draft regulations and local regulations, as well as draft amendments and additions to these acts.

General Legal Studies: Graduates of the program can carry out professional activities in such areas as: development and implementation of legal norms, ensuring law and order, and providing legal assistance to individuals and legal entities. The program includes relevant disciplines that reflect current trends in the legal services market and meet the needs of employers in this field. Along with fundamental theoretical training, students acquire skills in interpreting the norms of Russian law and applying Russian legislation in specific practical situations.
